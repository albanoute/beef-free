What will be done with your donation?

All of the money collected will be sent to help local farms in a country/ city of your choice. We only help eco-friendly farms, that take care of the animals and that will wisely spend your donation. This generous donation will help local farms to lower their prices so they can compete with the low prices of factory farms. Help us, Help animals, You can make the difference!
